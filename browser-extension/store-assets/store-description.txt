MindVault — Save any page to your personal visual reference library.

Built for filmmakers, DoPs, and visual creatives who collect references across the web. MindVault organizes your links into two spaces:

Eye — for visual inspiration (cinematography, photography, art direction)
Mind — for knowledge and ideas (articles, tutorials, research)

Features:

One-click save from any webpage directly into MindVault.

Smart space detection — YouTube, Vimeo, Instagram and other visual platforms auto-select Eye. GitHub, Medium, Wikipedia and knowledge sites auto-select Mind. You can always override manually.

Keyboard shortcuts for instant saving without opening the popup:
  Cmd+Shift+E saves to Eye
  Cmd+Shift+M saves to Mind

Connection status indicator shows whether MindVault is running on your Mac.

Minimal, distraction-free design that stays out of your way.

Requirements: MindVault desktop app must be running on the same computer (macOS). The extension communicates with MindVault locally — no data leaves your machine.

Privacy: This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All communication happens locally between the extension and the MindVault app on your computer.
