What it does
Capture the moment.

A frame that stops you. A colour palette, a scene, a cut that belongs in your next treatment. Save it in one move, from any source, any device. It's in your vault before the moment passes.

01
Always findable.

Collections, tags, and AI that recognises what you saved. No manual labelling, ever. Search by mood, colour, subject or concept. Your archive grows without becoming a mess.

02
Treatment-ready.

Trim a clip. Pull a still. Create a GIF. Your saved references become deliverables without leaving the app. Mood boards and treatments built from the material you collected from day one.

03

Two ways of seeing.

Not all inspiration works the same way. Some things you feel before you think them. Others you understand before you see them. MindVault keeps both worlds separate and searchable.

EYE

What you see.

Colour, light, texture, movement, composition. Visual references analysed for how they look. Find a frame by its feel, not by what you called it.

MIND

What you think.

Tutorials, behind-the-scenes, educational videos. The knowledge behind the craft, saved alongside your visual references, always in context.
